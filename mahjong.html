Rivertowns Mahjong Internship Project

I helped build out the brand for a mahjong business centered around bringing people together to play. The brand needed to feel modern and playful, so I focused on keeping everything cohesive across all the touchpoints. Ranging from social posts and signs to mahjong mats and logo studies. Every piece was designed to reflect that same fun, welcoming energy the brand is all about.
